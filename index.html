Let Me Introduce Myself

I am Prerana Pradhan passed out from Ryan International School with an A+ grade in 10th and 12th. Admitted to Gandhi Institute for Technological Advancement, Bhubaneswar to pursue Bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering. Scored 9+ GPA till date and right now inclined towards Tech and its Stacks. A volunteer web developer earning experience at India's top-most Women-in-Tech Foundation @GirlScriptFoundation. An experienced Marketing Manager with an experience of 2+ years.

Everything in this world has some or the other kind of possibilities, I had the worst ones. I was 13 when my father had his first fits attack, he was hospitalized in the city hospital. The doctor's said that he'll recover soon but everything went in vain. He was more into the disease day by day. Doctors gave him medicines that were not meant to be used for his treatment and later the side effects took him away.

It is not easy to live a life without a male head in dominant families of India. My mother took the entire responsibility of teaching me what I had interest in to what I wanted to become regardless of what my family wanted me to become. Everything we think of happening is not what happens accordingly, it happens what it is meant to be.

I am more than what people think of me and what I should really do in my life. I am a Nightingale with a voice that can infatuate you to get into me even more. I am a classical dancer with all the curves and postures a dancer must possess. I am an artist who love to design an entire new page of the upcoming chapters of my life. I am a woman who is not only excelled in house-hold activities but also a tech-maker.
